Once a promising fencer, now a filmmaker and performer—on screen with you.

Starting with this portfolio...

  Shell we go together? In this narrative, which appears to portray the idea of auteurism in a largely affirmative light, the fact that the overthrown power is named "Auteur" invites multiple layers of interpretation.
  One possible reading is that it functions as a satire on the way mass media indiscriminately overuses the term "artist." Another interpretation is that, even in a cultural landscape where a particular creative movement—such as auteurism—has become dominant, the work asserts the possibility of resisting full assimilation into the mainstream. Instead, it suggests that one can confront that dominance with confidence by twisting it through one's own distinct sensibility and voice.
